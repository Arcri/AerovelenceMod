Aerovelence is a large content mod themed around Space, with an interesting twist. Aerovelence has plenty of new content, new enemies to fight, and secrets to be uncovered.
If there are any issues with the mod, join the Discord server linked at the bottom and report bugs in #bug-reports.

Current rundown:
-200+ Items
-Large slew of new NPC's with fun and unique AI
-11 fun bossfights
-1 New event, the Fusion Storm!
-3 brand new biomes, The Forgotton/Shattered, Tartarus and the Crystal Fields!
-Legendary items that have a low drop rate from any boss, modded and vanilla alike!
